## JD (English)

### About Payments Group

Payments Group is a FinTech payment services provider headquartered in Singapore. We operate a regional payment network enabling cross-border QR payments and interoperable digital payment solutions for national networks, e-wallet operators, banks, acquirers, and merchants. Our mission is to deliver secure, seamless, and efficient digital payment experiences across Asia through deep ecosystem collaboration.

Through integrations with leading regional payment networks and digital wallet ecosystems, Liquid enables seamless cross-border QR payments across multiple markets, supporting both inbound and outbound transactions.

### Role Overview

We are seeking a highly strategic and commercially driven VP, Head of Growth & Activation to lead ecosystem growth, activation, transaction adoption, and partner participation across Liquid's Singapore market ecosystem and cross-border QR payment network.

This role will serve as the overall accountable lead across:
- Network Growth & Activation
- Merchant Acquiring (Direct)
- Growth Marketing & Enablement

within the ACT (Activation, Conversion & Transactions) function.

The successful candidate will oversee ecosystem participation growth across issuers, acquirers, merchants, and payment partners, while driving measurable transaction adoption, corridor expansion, and ecosystem activation outcomes across the SGQR+/RoamQR framework.

### Key Responsibilities

**1. Ecosystem Growth & Network Expansion**
- Define and lead ecosystem growth strategies across issuer, acquirer, merchant, and payment partner participation.
- Drive corridor expansion initiatives across domestic and cross-border payment ecosystems.
- Develop strategic frameworks to strengthen: ecosystem participation, transaction adoption, merchant acceptance, and network utilization.
- Build and manage strategic relationships with: issuers, acquirers, banks, wallets, payment networks, and ecosystem partners.
- Identify new growth opportunities across payment corridors, merchant ecosystems, and regional partnerships.

**2. Issuer, Acquirer & Merchant Ecosystem Leadership**
- Oversee growth and activation initiatives across issuer and acquiring ecosystems within Singapore.
- Drive merchant participation growth across both direct merchant acquiring and partner acquirer channels.
- Ensure ecosystem stakeholders are effectively activated to drive transaction growth and corridor utilization.
- Work closely with regional and overseas partners to align growth priorities and market activation initiatives.
- Support strategic expansion and participation growth of new payment partners and corridors.

**3. Transaction Growth & Ecosystem Activation**
- Own and drive transaction growth outcomes across the payment ecosystem.
- Lead activation strategies focused on: transaction adoption, active usage growth, repeat transaction behavior, corridor utilization, and merchant participation.
- Drive ecosystem-wide initiatives to increase transaction participation across domestic and cross-border payment flows.
- Work closely with Growth Marketing & Enablement teams to execute ecosystem campaigns, partner activations, and merchant visibility initiatives.
- Monitor ecosystem performance metrics to identify growth opportunities and underperforming segments.

**4. Growth Marketing & Campaign Leadership**
- Define ecosystem campaign priorities aligned to transaction growth and activation objectives.
- Oversee partner activation programs, ecosystem campaigns, merchant visibility initiatives, and usage growth activities.
- Coordinate with internal stakeholders and external agencies to support: partner campaigns, ecosystem marketing, POSM/decal initiatives, merchant activation programs, and market awareness efforts.
- Ensure all activation initiatives are commercially aligned and measurable against ecosystem growth KPIs.

**5. Cross-Functional Leadership & Ecosystem Alignment**
- Act as the central growth leader coordinating priorities across: Product Strategy & Solutions, Merchant Acquiring, Growth Marketing & Enablement, Ecosystem Enablement, Operations & Support, Technology & Network Operations.
- Ensure strong alignment between: commercial priorities, ecosystem participation, product capabilities, growth initiatives, activation objectives.
- Drive execution alignment across internal and external stakeholder groups to support scalable ecosystem growth.

### Requirements
- 12+ years of experience in payments, fintech, banking, digital wallets, or financial services.
- Strong experience leading: ecosystem growth, payment partnerships, transaction activation, merchant ecosystem expansion, cross-border payment initiatives.
- Strong understanding of: QR payment ecosystems, acquiring and issuer models, digital wallets, payment networks, merchant acceptance ecosystems, transaction growth drivers.
- Proven ability to drive measurable: transaction growth, ecosystem participation, merchant activation, partner adoption outcomes.
- Strong commercial acumen with strategic growth leadership capabilities.
- Experience managing cross-functional ecosystem initiatives across: commercial, marketing, product, operations, and regional partner stakeholders.

- Data-driven with strong analytical, reporting, and performance management skills.
- Experience engaging with: banks, acquirers, payment networks, national payment schemes, digital wallets, regional ecosystem partners is highly preferred.
- Experience in regional or cross-border payment ecosystems will be highly advantageous.

## JD (中文)

### 关于 Payments Group

Payments Group 是一家总部位于新加坡的金融科技支付服务提供商。我们运营一个区域支付网络，为国家网络、电子钱包运营商、银行、收单机构和商户提供跨境 QR 支付和互操作数字支付解决方案。我们的使命是通过深度的生态系统协作，在亚洲范围内提供安全、无缝、高效的数字支付体验。

### 职位概述

我们正在寻找一位高度战略性、商业驱动的 VP，增长与激活负责人，负责领导 Liquid 新加坡市场生态及跨境 QR 支付网络的生态增长、激活、交易采纳和合作伙伴参与。

该角色将作为 ACT（激活、转化与交易）职能的整体负责人，统管：
- 网络增长与激活
- 商户收单（直营）
- 增长营销与赋能

成功的候选人将推动发卡行、收单行、商户和支付合作伙伴的生态参与增长，同时在 SGQR+/RoamQR 框架下实现可衡量的交易采纳、通道扩展和生态激活成果。

### 核心职责

**1. 生态增长与网络扩展**
- 制定并领导发卡方、收单方、商户及支付合作伙伴的生态增长策略
- 推动国内和跨境支付生态的通道扩展计划
- 构建战略框架以强化生态参与、交易采纳、商户受理和网络利用率
- 建立并管理与发卡行、收单行、银行、钱包、支付网络及生态合作伙伴的战略关系

**2. 发卡方、收单方与商户生态领导力**
- 统筹新加坡市场发卡和收单生态的增长与激活计划
- 通过直营商户收单和合作收单渠道双轮驱动商户参与增长
- 确保生态利益相关方被有效激活，以推动交易增长和通道利用率

**3. 交易增长与生态激活**
- 全面负责支付生态的交易增长成果
- 领导激活策略，聚焦交易采纳、活跃使用增长、复购行为、通道利用率、商户参与
- 推动生态级计划，提升国内和跨境支付流中的交易参与度

**4. 增长营销与活动领导力**
- 定义与交易增长和激活目标对齐的生态营销活动优先级
- 统筹合作伙伴激活计划、生态营销活动、商户曝光计划及使用增长活动

**5. 跨职能领导力与生态协同**
- 作为核心增长负责人，统筹协调产品策略、商户收单、增长营销、生态赋能、运营与技术等各方优先级
- 确保商业优先级、生态参与、产品能力、增长计划和激活目标之间的高度一致

### 任职要求
- 12 年以上支付、金融科技、银行、数字钱包或金融服务领域经验
- 具备领导生态增长、支付合作伙伴关系、交易激活、商户生态扩展、跨境支付计划的丰富经验
- 深刻理解 QR 支付生态、收单和发卡模式、数字钱包、支付网络、商户受理生态、交易增长驱动因素
- 强大的商业敏锐度和战略增长领导能力
- 有区域或跨境支付生态经验者优先
